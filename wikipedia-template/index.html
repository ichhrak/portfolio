Ichrak El Hatimi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Name: Ichrak El Hatimi

Email: hatimiichhrak@gmail.com

Phone: (514) 796-4525

Ichrak El Hatimi (born June 4th 2002) is a highly driven software engineering student at the University of Ottawa, set to graduate in April 2025. Known for her strong academic foundation and practical experience in software engineering, she has completed multiple internships at leading global companies, including Morgan Stanley, Motorola Solutions and Eurafric Information.

She has developed a strong foundation in backend technologies, including Java, SpringBoot, PostgreSQL, and ElasticSearch, through both university studies and internships. These experiences have allowed her to build and optimize scalable systems and automate complex processes. On the frontend side, she has worked with React.js and Bootstrap, applying these technologies to various university projects, which can be found on her Github . Passionate about both technical problem-solving and collaboration, Ichrak is eager to contribute to innovative teams that value creativity and teamwork.
